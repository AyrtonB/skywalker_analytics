Analysis


We're excited to announce the launch of our brand new flagship product, the Smart Meter Insights Studio. We've been working with Tatooine Energy to reduce their operational costs and further their customer knowledge.

On Tatooine, as with any binary system, the high variance in solar power and uneven generation cycles require more grid flexibility. The vaporators used by the planet's moisture farmers consume large amounts of energy on a constant basis, meaning flexibility must come from domestic users rather than industry.

Our collaboration with Tatooine Energy has included the design of their new Time-of-Use tariff aimed at displacing energy consumption away from peak times. Central to this has been the identification of households who are most likely to respond to the tariffs, we've achieved this through analysis of their historical smart meter data.
